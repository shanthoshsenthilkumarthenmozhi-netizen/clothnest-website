ABOUT CLOTHNEST

Your Trusted Garment Buying Office Since 2024

Founded in 2024 in Tiruppur, ClothNest connects global buyers with reliable garment manufacturers. We specialize in sourcing, quality control, production follow-ups, and compliance monitoring to ensure seamless and transparent execution.

From design coordination to final shipment, we ensure every garment meets international standards while maintaining ethical and sustainable practices across the supply chain.

OUR SERVICES

End-to-End Garment Buying Solutions

ClothNest provides complete buying office services, supporting buyers from product development to final shipment with transparency, quality control, and timely execution.

Vendor Sourcing

Identifying and onboarding reliable garment manufacturers based on buyer requirements, capacity, compliance, and quality standards.

Quality Control & Inspection

Inline and final inspections to ensure garments meet buyer specifications, quality standards, and compliance requirements.

Product Development

Coordinating sampling, fabric selection, trims, and design execution based on buyer tech packs or mood boards.

Production Monitoring

Continuous production follow-ups to ensure correct execution, timely output, and smooth coordination between buyers and factories.

Compliance & Audits

Ensuring factories follow social, safety, and ethical compliance standards required by international buyers.

Shipment Coordination

Coordinating packing, documentation, and shipment schedules to ensure smooth and timely delivery to destination ports.
